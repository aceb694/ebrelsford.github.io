why use mapbox.js rather than point to the tiles directly?

Mapbox.js takes care of attribution for you

Mapbox.js takes care of access tokens for you

more importantly, Mapbox.js automatically loads higher-res tiles for retina devices

since Mapbox.js wraps Leaflet, you only have to include Mapbox.js
